Importance Of Wild Life

Wild life plays a cruxial role in maintainin cological balance and supporting life on Earth. Different species interact within ecosystems to regulate food chains, pollinate plants, and maintain soil and water health. Protecting biodiversity ensures that natural systems continue to function effectively and sustainably (Guo, 2025).
